Originally, I was going to play the clarinet like my cousin, yet I realized that I should not be following someone and rather make a decision of my own.
Via private lessons and hours of practicing, I was always one of the top students in the orchestra. I love Back due to the several different emotions he demonstrates in his pieces. I hope to continue playing the violin into the future, maybe not via orchestra, but just for fun.
I have made several lifelong friends via orchestra. I have met several people of different cultures and backgrounds. Itt really has been a great experience.
